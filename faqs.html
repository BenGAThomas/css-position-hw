The GameFAQs FAQ(Frequently Asked Questions)

What is GameFAQs?

GameFAQs, once known as the Video Game FAQ Archive, is an archive of FAQs, move lists, walkthroughs, codes, and other hints and tips for Arcade, Computer, and Home Video games. It first came online on November 5, 1995, and has been dutifully serving the Internet gaming community ever since.
